Programme Overview

Building Futures Through Digital Empowerment

Through comprehensive training sessions, mentorship programmes, and strategic partnerships with industry players and educational institutions, MTA enhances employability and cultivates a proactive, innovative mindset.

🎯 Secure Employment Pathways

Provide underserved youth with comprehensive digital and career development training to secure meaningful employment.

Indicator: At least 90% of participants will secure career-fitting employment above RM2,000 through increased employability and confidence.

🌱 Foster Long-Term Resilience

Build resilience and growth mindset through community building, peer support, and mentorship at every stage.

Indicator: Facilitate 10 peer support gatherings per month and achieve at least 70% increase in participants reporting enhanced growth mindset.
